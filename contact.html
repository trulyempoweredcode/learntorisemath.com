What to Expect

  1. 1

    You Send a Message

    Use the form on this page. Tell me a little about your child's experience with maths. There are no wrong answers.

  2. 2

    I Respond Within Two Working Days

    I'll get back to you by email to arrange a convenient time for a phone or video conversation.

  3. 3

    We Have a Relaxed Conversation

    This is not a sales call. It's a genuine, two-way discussion to explore whether this approach is the right fit for your child.

    • Free and no obligation
    • Relaxed and unhurried
    • Focused on your child's experience
    • Honest about whether I can help
  4. 4

    We Decide Together

    If we both feel it's a good fit, I'll recommend a programme and we'll discuss next steps. If not, I'll always say so and may suggest alternatives.
