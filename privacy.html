Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: 13 March 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Learn to Rise collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you visit this website or use my tutoring services. I am committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

By using this website or engaging my services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is:

  • Name: Learn to Rise
  • Email: hello@learntorisemaths.com
  • Website: learntorisemaths.com

Learn to Rise is operated as a sole trader. If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact me using the details above.

3. What Information I Collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

When you use the contact form or engage my services, I may collect:

  • Your name (parent or guardian)
  • Your email address
  • Your child's first name and age
  • The programme you are interested in
  • Any additional information you include in your message
  • Session notes and progress records created during the course of tutoring

3.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit this website, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type and operating system

This information is collected through analytics tools and is used to understand how visitors use the website so I can improve it. See section 9 for more on cookies.

4. How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and arrange an initial conversation
  • Provide tutoring services to your child
  • Maintain session notes and track your child's progress
  • Communicate with you about sessions, scheduling, and progress
  • Improve the website and the services I offer
  • Comply with legal obligations

I will never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for purposes unrelated to the services described above.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK GDPR, I rely on the following legal bases to process your personal data:

  • Consent: When you submit the contact form or agree to your child receiving tutoring services. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting me.
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary to deliver the tutoring services you have engaged.
  • Legitimate interests: For website analytics and improving my services, where this does not override your rights and freedoms.
  • Legal obligation: Where I am required to retain certain records by law.

6. How I Store and Protect Your Data

I take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Measures include:

  • Storing digital records securely with password protection and encryption where appropriate
  • Limiting access to personal data to myself only
  • Using secure, reputable third-party services for email and file storage
  • Ensuring any third-party processors I use comply with UK data protection standards

I do not store payment card details. All payments are processed through separate, secure payment methods, and I do not have access to your card information.

7. How Long I Keep Your Data

I retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to 12 months after the last communication, unless you engage my services.
  • Client records and session notes: Retained for up to 3 years after the tutoring relationship ends, in case they are needed for reference or follow-up.
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymised form as determined by the analytics provider's settings.

You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time (see section 10).

8. Sharing Your Data

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. I may share limited data with the following types of third parties, only where necessary:

  • Website hosting provider: To deliver this website securely.
  • Email service provider: To manage communications.
  • Analytics provider: To collect anonymised website usage data.

All third-party providers I use are based in the UK or comply with UK data protection requirements for international data transfers. I do not share session notes or your child's information with any third party without your explicit consent.

9. Cookies

This website uses cookies, small text files placed on your device, to help me understand how visitors use the site.

The cookies used on this site include:

  • Analytics cookies: These collect anonymised information about how you use the website, such as which pages you visit and how long you spend on them. This helps me improve the site.
  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function correctly (for example, remembering your cookie preferences).

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience of the website. For more information about cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

10. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data I hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask me to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask me to delete your personal data where there is no compelling reason for me to continue processing it.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask me to limit how I use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You can request that I provide your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data where I rely on legitimate interests as the legal basis.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at hello@learntorisemaths.com. I will respond to your request within one month.

11. Children's Privacy

Learn to Rise provides tutoring services to children aged 9–16. I take the privacy of children very seriously.

  • I only collect children's data (such as first name, age, and session notes) with the consent of a parent or guardian.
  • I do not collect data directly from children through the website. All contact form submissions should be made by a parent or guardian.
  • Session notes and progress records relating to a child are shared only with the parent or guardian who engaged the service.
  • Parents and guardians may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time.

12. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to external websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. I encourage you to read the privacy policy of any website you visit.

13. Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in how I handle personal data or to comply with legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how I have handled your personal data, I would appreciate the chance to address your concerns. Please contact me at hello@learntorisemaths.com in the first instance.

If you remain dissatisfied, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Telephone: 0303 123 1113
  • Post: Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF

15. Contact Me

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how I handle your personal data, please get in touch:

  • Email: hello@learntorisemaths.com
  • Website: learntorisemaths.com/contact
