Gold Package

Specialist Mathematical Development Programme

A structured, specialist pathway for learners who need a more thoughtful and carefully paced approach

This programme is for learners who need more than traditional tutoring. Rather than focusing solely on curriculum coverage, the work centres on confidence, thinking processes and the gradual development of mathematical understanding over time. Sessions are typically 25–40 minutes to maintain focus and reduce cognitive overload.

Three structured stages:

Stage 1 — Diagnostic & Foundations

£1,800–£2,000 /month

Approx. $2,450–$2,700 USD

One-month intensive: up to 5 sessions/week (25–40 min each). Diagnostic assessment, identification of barriers, early confidence-building, and parent consultation.

Stage 2 — Development Phase

£1,000–£1,200 /month

Approx. $1,350–$1,650 USD

Up to 3 sessions/week. Rebuilding mathematical understanding, personalised learning strategies, and ongoing review. May continue for several months.

Stage 3 — Consolidation

£500–£700 /month

Approx. $675–$945 USD

Up to 2 sessions/week. Strengthening independence, consolidating key ideas, and support with school assessments where appropriate.

Movement between stages is based on the learner’s readiness rather than a fixed timetable. Progress is reviewed every 6–8 weeks. Support can be adjusted at any time.

What's Behind the Investment

Each week involves far more than the session itself. For every hour of 1:1 time with your child, there are hours of preparation, review, and communication behind the scenes.

Before each session

Reviewing progress, preparing personalised materials, adapting plans based on the previous session.

During each session

Focused 1:1 online teaching, tailored to your child's pace, needs, and emotional readiness.

Between sessions

Agreed support, resource preparation, and evidence-informed planning for long-term progress.

Ongoing

Regular parent consultations, progress reviews, and continuous professional reflection.

Sessions focus on rebuilding understanding step by step, developing confidence and helping students engage with mathematics in a calmer and more positive way.

When appropriate, sessions may also include support with school tests and exams, but the primary focus remains on building strong mathematical understanding.
