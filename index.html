If Maths Has Become a Source of Stress, You're in the Right Place

Many of the learners I work with have been achieving below expectation and quietly losing belief in their ability, or have been defined by their struggles for too long. They're dealing with pressure, overwhelm.

I offer a different experience. Through carefully structured 1:1 online sessions, your child gets the time and space to think clearly, ask questions safely, and build genuine understanding at a pace that works for them. I do not give up on learners. We stay with the difficulty until it shifts.

A Different Approach

A Different Approach to Maths Support

This is not high-volume tutoring or last-minute exam cramming.

Sessions focus on reducing cognitive overload, strengthening reasoning, and rebuilding confidence through clarity and structure. The emphasis is always on secure, transferable understanding, not short-term performance.

Every session is shaped by over twenty years of experience in secondary mathematics education, including leadership, teacher training, and postgraduate research at the University of Cambridge into how learning environments affect student outcomes.
